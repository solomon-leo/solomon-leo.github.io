About Me

Hello! My name is Solomon Leo and I am a fourth year student at the Uniersity of Texas at Austin studying Chemical Engineering.

I am interested in applying data driven solutions to engineering problems in electrical, chemical, and biomedical engineering. I am currently interning at AMD as a Foundry Technology Operations Co-op.

I am also involved in undergraduate research in the Akinwande Nano Research Group at UT Austin where I work on data processing for RF sensors and developing machine learning models to accurately predict blood pressure. You can find some of my favorite projects in the projects tab! In my free time I like to paddleboard on the Colorado River, ski, and cook with my friends.
